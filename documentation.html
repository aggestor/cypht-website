Archive Email

Cypht offers two way to archive message, you can archive message from your message or from reading your message. Here are the steps of how to do it :

  1. Click the on square and after clicking, the square will have a gray background which means the message is selected and the menu will appear on the top of the page
  2. After the menu appear click the archive button as shown on the screen shot then, the message will move to the archive folder.

  1. Here as you can see on the screenshot, you read the message and after reading it, find the achive message link, click it and the message will be archived.
